About Zwane Financial Services

What we stand for

Zwane Financial Services is a 100% black-owned, registered credit provider and FSP. We are committed to providing individuals and businesses who are financially excluded with easy and secure access to credit. We take pride in our dedication to serving and empowering low-income and underserved communities, while actively working to bridge the wealth gap by offering reliable financial services to disadvantaged populations.

Our Strict Code of Conduct:
  • Pre-Agreement Statement and Quotation: We provide all clients with a clear pre-agreement statement and a comprehensive quotation prior to entering into any agreement.
  • Credit Agreement Transparency: We ensure that all clients receive a copy of their credit agreement, detailing the terms and conditions in an easily understandable format.
  • Full Disclosure of Costs: We inform clients of all costs associated with the agreement, ensuring they are fully aware of their financial commitments.
  • Credit Reporting: We adhere to the NCR, FSCA and FIC as required, maintaining the highest standards of compliance.
  • Client Support in Default Situations: We assist clients who find themselves in default, ensuring they have the support needed to navigate difficult financial situations.

Our story

In South Africa, a significant portion of the population is excluded from formal banking systems and microfinance institutions. As a result, many financially disqualified individuals fall prey to loan sharks and unscrupulous credit providers who impose exorbitant interest rates, trapping them in a cycle of debt and further marginalizing them from the mainstream economy.

This is the driving force behind the establishment of Zwane Financial Services. We recognized the need to empower individuals who lack access to essential economic services and, as a result, we offer a variety of quality and affordable financial products designed to meet their needs and help them build a better financial future.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will I be eligible for an increase in my loan amount?
Zwane Financial Services has a policy requiring all new clients to take at least three one-month loans, based on their affordability and credit report. After completing these three loans, we begin to consider increasing the loan amount and terms.
Does your financing require upfront payments?
We will never ask for an upfront fee. That is illegal. Please contact us should you be required to pay an upfront fee by any of our consultants.
Can I use my SRD SASSA grant to apply for financing?
No.
How do I lodge a complaint?
Email admin@zwanefin.co.za or contact 069 119 5046.
Which bank accounts do you accept?
All major bank accounts, except Discovery.
Tymebank applications are processed in branch.
